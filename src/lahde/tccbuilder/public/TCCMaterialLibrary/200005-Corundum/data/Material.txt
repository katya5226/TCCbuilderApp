Al2O3 Aluminum oxide in the form of Corundum

Melting point is around 2300 K (https://www.britannica.com/science/corundum, 28/09/2023)

Data for density and specific heat capacity at RT were proveided by Electronic Ceramics Department »Jožef Stefan« Institute
And was also measured in our lab (LAHE, FS, UNI-LJ). This data is not publicly available.

Thermal conductivity was measured in our lab (LAHE, FS, UNI-LJ). This data is not publicly available.

Emissivity at RT can be found for example at https://www.omega.co.uk/literature/transactions/volume1/emissivityb.html (28/09/2023).
